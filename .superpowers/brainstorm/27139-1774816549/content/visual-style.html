3D Forest Visual Style

How should commits look when they "grow" into the forest?

A — Pure Voxel Bars

Faithful to the original video. GitHub's green palette, cubes extrude upward by commit count. Minimal, geometric, immediately recognizable. Clean and fast to render.

B — Voxel Trees

Same blocky aesthetic but cubes form tree silhouettes — layered pyramids with brown trunks. Taller trees = more commits. Minecraft forest vibes. More whimsical and shareable.

C — Low-Poly Forest

Triangular pine trees with layered canopies. More organic and "forest-like." Height and density scale with commits. Beautiful but more complex geometry.
